Quantum computing is a new type of computing that uses principles from quantum mechanics, like superposition and entanglement, to process information. * **Classical computers** use **bits** that are either a 0 or a 1. * **Quantum computers** use **qubits** which can be 0, 1, or both at the same time (this is called **superposition**). * **Entanglement** allows qubits to be linked so that the state of one instantly affects the others, even when separated. These quantum phenomena allow quantum computers to perform complex calculations much faster for specific problems that are too difficult for traditional computers, such as drug discovery, materials science, and cryptography.
